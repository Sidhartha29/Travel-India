Medaram Sammakka Saralamma Temple

The Medaram Sammakka Saralamma Temple, located in the Mulugu district of Telangana, is a sacred site dedicated to the tribal goddesses Sammakka and Saralamma. This temple is at the heart of the Sammakka Saralamma Jatara, one of the largest tribal festivals in the world.

Held every two years, the Sammakka Saralamma Jatara attracts millions of devotees who come to pay homage to the warrior goddesses. The festival is known for its unique rituals, deep-rooted tribal traditions, and the gathering of indigenous communities from across India.
