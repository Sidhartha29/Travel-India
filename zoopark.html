ZOO PARK HYD

Nehru Zoological Park, located in Hyderabad, is one of the largest and most well-maintained zoos in India. It is home to a wide variety of animal species, making it a popular destination for wildlife enthusiasts and families.

The zoo features spacious enclosures, a safari park, and a dedicated conservation program. Visitors can witness exotic animals like tigers, elephants, and giraffes, along with a rich collection of birds and reptiles.

With its lush green surroundings, well-planned exhibits, and educational programs, Nehru Zoological Park is a must-visit attraction in Hyderabad for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts.
