Bhadrachalam

Bhadrachalam, a prominent pilgrimage site in Telangana, is known for the famous Sri Sita Ramachandra Swamy Temple, dedicated to Lord Rama.

Situated on the banks of the Godavari River, Bhadrachalam holds immense religious significance and is deeply connected to the Ramayana.

The town is a spiritual retreat, attracting devotees and travelers seeking peace, devotion, and divine blessings.
