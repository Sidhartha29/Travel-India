Peacock Park, Yercaud

A sanctuary of natural beauty and vibrant wildlife

About Peacock Park

Peacock Park is a charming attraction in Yercaud, Tamil Nadu, known for its vibrant population of peacocks that roam freely in a lush, green environment. This park is a perfect place for nature lovers, photographers, and wildlife enthusiasts to witness the beauty of these magnificent birds in their natural habitat.

History

Established to conserve the peacock population in the region, Peacock Park has grown into a popular tourist spot over the years. It serves as a sanctuary for these majestic birds and offers visitors an opportunity to observe their graceful dance and colorful plumage up close.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Peacock Park is during the early morning or late afternoon when the peacocks are most active. The monsoon and winter seasons offer the most picturesque views, with lush greenery and mist-covered landscapes.
