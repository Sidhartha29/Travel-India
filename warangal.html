Warangal

Warangal, the historical capital of the Kakatiya dynasty, is a city in Telangana known for its rich heritage and architectural marvels.

The city is home to the iconic Warangal Fort, the stunning Thousand Pillar Temple, and the beautiful Ramappa Temple, which is recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

With its blend of history, culture, and scenic beauty, Warangal is a must-visit destination for history enthusiasts and travelers seeking to explore ancient wonders.
