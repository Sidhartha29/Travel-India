Lambasingi

Lambasingi, often referred to as the 'Kashmir of Andhra Pradesh,' is a picturesque hill station in the Visakhapatnam district. It is the only place in South India that experiences sub-zero temperatures during winter.

Surrounded by dense forests, misty hills, and coffee plantations, Lambasingi is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers. The region is also home to various tribal communities, adding to its cultural charm.

The cool climate, scenic landscapes, and breathtaking sunrise views make Lambasingi a must-visit destination for travelers looking for a tranquil escape in the lap of nature.
