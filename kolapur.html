Kolhapur - The City of Goddess Mahalaxmi

Kolhapur

Kolhapur is a historic city in Maharashtra, often referred to as **"Dakshin Kashi"** due to its deep-rooted spiritual and cultural significance. It is home to the famous **Mahalaxmi Temple**, one of the most revered Hindu pilgrimage sites. The city is known for its **Kolhapuri chappals**, **Kolhapuri cuisine**, and **wrestling culture (Kushti)**. It was once the capital of the **Kolhapur princely state**, ruled by the Bhosale Chhatrapatis of the Maratha Empire. Some must-visit places include **New Palace, Rankala Lake, Panhala Fort, and Jyotiba Temple**. The city beautifully blends history, spirituality, and modern development, making it a unique travel destination.
