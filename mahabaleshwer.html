Mahabaleshwar

Mahabaleshwar, a beautiful hill station in Maharashtra, is famous for its scenic beauty, lush greenery, and cool climate. Located in the Western Ghats, it is home to breathtaking viewpoints, strawberry farms, and ancient temples. Some of the must-visit places include Arthur’s Seat, Venna Lake, Pratapgad Fort, and Elephant’s Head Point. Mahabaleshwar is also well known for its delicious strawberries, fresh air, and serene landscapes, making it a perfect getaway for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.
