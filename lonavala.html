Lonavala

Lonavala, located in the Pune district of Maharashtra, is a popular hill station known for its scenic beauty, waterfalls, and tranquil atmosphere. Nestled amidst the Sahyadri mountain range, it is a popular weekend getaway from Mumbai and Pune.

Famous for its lush green landscapes, misty hills, and historical forts, Lonavala offers a refreshing escape. The town is known for the Bhushi Dam, Tigerâ€™s Leap, and Karla Caves, among other tourist spots. During the monsoon season, Lonavala becomes even more beautiful, with its waterfalls cascading down the hills, and the misty weather creating a magical experience.

Lonavala is also famous for its chikki, a traditional Indian sweet made from jaggery and peanuts, which visitors can enjoy while taking in the serene surroundings.
