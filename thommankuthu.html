Thommankuthu Waterfall

Thommankuthu Waterfall, located in the Idukki district of Kerala, is a mesmerizing seven-step waterfall surrounded by dense forests. Known for its breathtaking natural beauty, it is a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

The cascading waterfall, with pools at different levels, offers an enchanting trekking experience through lush green forests. The region is rich in flora and fauna, making it an ideal spot for eco-tourism and wildlife photography.

Thommankuthu is a must-visit for those looking to explore the raw, untouched beauty of Kerala’s landscape. The fresh air, soothing sound of flowing water, and picturesque scenery create a truly rejuvenating experience.
