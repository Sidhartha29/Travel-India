Eravikulam National Park

Eravikulam National Park, located in the Western Ghats of Kerala, is known for its rolling grasslands, mist-covered hills, and the endangered Nilgiri Tahr. The park is a must-visit for wildlife lovers and nature enthusiasts.

One of the most famous attractions of Eravikulam is the blooming of the rare Neelakurinji flowers, which turn the hills into a sea of blue once every 12 years. The park also offers breathtaking views of the Anamudi Peak, the highest point in South India.

Visitors can experience an unforgettable journey through misty trails, lush greenery, and the vibrant wildlife that makes Eravikulam a truly special place.
