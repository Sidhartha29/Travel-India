Nagarjuna Sagar Dam

Nagarjuna Sagar Dam is a masonry dam across the Krishna River at Nagarjuna Sagar which straddles the border between Nalgonda district in Telangana and Palnadu district in Andhra Pradesh. The dam provides irrigation water to multiple districts and also serves as a source of electricity generation.

History

The project's construction was officially inaugurated by Prime Minister Jawaharlal Nehru on 10 December 1955 and proceeded for the next twelve years. The dam was one of the earliest multi-purpose irrigation and hydroelectric projects in India.

In 2015, the diamond jubilee celebrations of the project's inauguration were held, marking the prosperity and development it has brought to the region.
