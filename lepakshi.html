Lepakshi

Lepakshi is a small town in the Anantapur district of Andhra Pradesh, India, renowned for its historical and cultural significance. It is home to the famous Veerabhadra Temple, which showcases intricate Vijayanagara-style architecture.

The temple is known for its beautifully carved pillars, the famous Hanging Pillar, and the massive Nandi statue located nearby. Lepakshi is steeped in mythology and is believed to be the place where Jatayu fell while trying to rescue Sita in the Ramayana.
