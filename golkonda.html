Golconda Fort, Hyderabad

Golconda Fort is one of the most prominent historical landmarks of Hyderabad. Known for its majestic structure and rich history, it was once the capital of the Qutb Shahi dynasty. The fort was built in the 16th century and boasts impressive architecture and a strategic location on a hilltop.
