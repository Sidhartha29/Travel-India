Alibaug is a popular coastal destination located in the Raigad district of Maharashtra. Known for its serene beaches, historic forts, and tranquil atmosphere, it is a perfect getaway from the bustling city life of Mumbai, just a short ferry ride away.

With its clean sandy beaches like Alibaug Beach and Nagaon Beach, Alibaug is a haven for beach lovers, water sports enthusiasts, and anyone looking for a peaceful escape. The town also boasts of historic landmarks like the Kolaba Fort, which is situated in the sea and can be accessed during low tide.

Whether it's enjoying the sunset on a beach, exploring the ancient forts, or simply relaxing in the natural beauty of the surroundings, Alibaug offers a refreshing coastal experience.
