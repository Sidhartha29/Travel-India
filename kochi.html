Kochi

History

Kochi is a coastal city located in the Ernakulam District in the Indian state of Kerala about 200 km from Thiruvananthapuram, the capital of Kerala. For many centuries, Kochi was the seat of the eponymous princely state. Muziris, a center of global trade, traces its history back many centuries as a hub for Indian spice trade.

Prehistory

Not much is known about the prehistory of Kochi. The only trace of prehistoric life in the region is the menhir found in Tripunithura.
