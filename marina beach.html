Marina Beach, Chennai

Marina Beach, located in Chennai, is the longest natural urban beach in India, stretching along the Bay of Bengal for 13 km. It is one of the most popular attractions in Tamil Nadu.

Known for its golden sands, gentle waves, and breathtaking sunrises, Marina Beach offers a refreshing escape for tourists and locals alike. The beach is lined with food stalls, statues, and historic landmarks.

From horse rides to kite flying, Marina Beach is a perfect spot for families, photographers, and adventure lovers. The cool sea breeze and lively atmosphere make it a must-visit destination in Chennai.

Location: Marina Beach is located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. The coordinates are 13.0676° N latitude and 80.2829° E longitude.
