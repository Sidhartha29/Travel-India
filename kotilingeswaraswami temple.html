Kotilingeshwara Swamy Temple

Kotilingeshwara Swamy Temple, located in Karnataka, is a unique temple famous for housing one of the largest Shiva Lingams in the world. The temple derives its name from the goal of installing one crore (ten million) Shiva Lingams within its premises.
