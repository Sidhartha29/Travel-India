Karjat

Karjat is a picturesque town located in the Raigad district of Maharashtra. Surrounded by lush greenery, rolling hills, and beautiful waterfalls, Karjat is a perfect escape for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

The town is renowned for its hiking trails, forts like the Kondana Caves, and scenic beauty. Karjat is also a popular weekend getaway from Mumbai, offering activities such as trekking, river rafting, and camping. The region is especially famous during the monsoon season when the waterfalls come alive and the landscape turns a vibrant green.

Whether you're looking to trek through the hills, visit the ancient forts, or simply unwind in nature's lap, Karjat offers a refreshing experience filled with natural charm.
