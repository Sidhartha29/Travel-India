Kinnerasani Wildlife Sanctuary

Kinnerasani Wildlife Sanctuary, located in the Bhadradri Kothagudem district of Telangana, is a stunning reserve known for its rich biodiversity and scenic beauty. The sanctuary is named after the Kinnerasani River, which flows through its heart.

Home to diverse flora and fauna, including deer, sloth bears, leopards, and a variety of bird species, Kinnerasani offers a perfect retreat for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts. The dense forests and the picturesque Kinnerasani reservoir add to its charm.

The breathtaking landscapes, tranquil water bodies, and the serene ambiance make Kinnerasani Wildlife Sanctuary a must-visit destination for those seeking peace in the lap of nature.
