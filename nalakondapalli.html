Nelakondapalli, Telangana

Nelakondapalli is a historic site located in the Khammam district of Telangana. This archaeological site is famous for its ancient Budhist remains and artifacts, which date back to the 3rd to 5th century CE. The place has significant cultural value, showcasing the region's ancient Buddhist heritage.

Nelakondapalli is well-known for the discovery of numerous Buddhist sculptures and inscriptions that provide insights into the ancient Buddhist civilization that once thrived in this area. The site offers a rare glimpse into the historical development of Buddhism in South India.

Visitors can explore the ruins, including ancient stupas and remnants of monasteries, while learning about the Buddhist way of life that prevailed in this region centuries ago.
